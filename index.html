Listening test

Dear testee, you are here for the important task of benchmarking the performance of rirnet, a machine learning system that analyzes reverberant sound. Rirnet listens to a sound and tries to mimic the influence that the room had on it, this creates a transfer function. The transfer function can then be used to create new, synthetic sounds that sound as if they belong in the targeted soundscape.

Below are ten different room settings with two sounds each. One is a real sound, one is a synthesized impostor. Your job is to listen to the sounds and select the impostor for each room and then click submit at the end. We highly recommend wearing headphones.

Thank you for your participation!
