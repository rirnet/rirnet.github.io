Test 1: Matching sounds with similar characteristics

Dear testee, you are here for the important task of benchmarking the performance of rirnet.

Below are ten different room settings with three sounds each. In the top of the three is a reference sound. The bottom two has one real sound which is from the same room as the reference one, and one synthesized version of it. Your job is to listen to the three sounds and select the sound in the second row with the most similar characteristics to the reference sound in the top. Do this for each room and then click submit at the end. We highly recommend wearing headphones.

Thank you for your participation!

/Erik Larsson and Felix Wiberg
