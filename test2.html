Test 2: Matching images and sounds

Dear testee, you are here for the important task of benchmarking the performance of rirnet.

Below are ten different room settings with two sounds each. One is a real sound, one is a synthesized version of it. Your job is look at the photo of the room and try to imagine how sounds would naturally sound in it. Then listen to the two related sounds and select the sound that you think seems natural. Do this for each room and then click submit at the end. We highly recommend wearing headphones.

Thank you for your participation!
